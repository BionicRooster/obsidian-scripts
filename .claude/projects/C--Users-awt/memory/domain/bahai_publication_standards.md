**Rule:** When working with Bahá'í publications, use fully marked diacritical forms. Correct misspellings on sight.

**Why:** The Bahá'í World News Service style guide and academic Bahá'í publications require precise transliteration. Missing diacriticals are errors, not stylistic choices.

**How to apply:** During any vault workflow touching Bahá'í content (classify, crosslink, synthesis update, biography, transcript), verify and fix spellings against this reference.

---

## Central Figures — Correct Forms

| Name | Correct form | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| The Báb | `the Báb` | Always with article; capital B; personal name: Siyyid 'Alí-Muḥammad |
| Bahá'u'lláh | `Bahá'u'lláh` | No article; personal name: Mírzá Ḥusayn-'Alí Núrí (1817–1892) |
| 'Abdu'l-Bahá | `'Abdu'l-Bahá` | No article; note opening `'` (ayn); given name: 'Abbás |
| Shoghi Effendi | `Shoghi Effendi` | No diacriticals needed; Guardian of the Bahá'í Faith |
| Universal House of Justice | `Universal House of Justice` | No article needed |

## Family Terminology

| Term | Correct form | Meaning |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Afnán | `Afnán` | Relatives/kin of the Báb |
| Aghsán | `Aghsán` | Male descendants of Bahá'u'lláh ("Branches") |
| Greatest Holy Leaf | `Bahíyyih Khánum, the Greatest Holy Leaf` | Daughter of Bahá'u'lláh |
| Most Great Branch | `'Abdu'l-Bahá, the Most Great Branch` | Covenantal title |

## Capitalization Rules

- Capitalize titles functioning as names: "the Báb," "Bahá'u'lláh," "'Abdu'l-Bahá," "Greatest Holy Leaf," "Most Great Branch"
- Capitalize the Faith and community: "Bahá'í Faith," "Bahá'ís," "Bábí movement"
- Do NOT capitalize pronouns (He, His, etc.) in academic/vault prose — this is devotional usage only
- Do NOT use devotional epithets ("Blessed Beauty," "Ancient Beauty") outside quoted sources
