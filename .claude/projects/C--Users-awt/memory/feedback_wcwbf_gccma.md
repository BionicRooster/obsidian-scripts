Any note tagged with or primarily about WCWBF (Willie Hall Center / Willie C. Webb Foundation) must be linked in the `## Georgetown Cultural Citizens Memorial Association` section of MOC - Home & Practical Life, in addition to wherever else it is classified.

**Why:** WCWBF is an arm of GCCMA — they are the same community organization. User confirmed this as a standing rule when WCWBF Steering Committee Meeting was moved out of Practical Tips to GCCMA only.

**How to apply:** During classify or MOC audit, if a note mentions WCWBF or is about the Willie Hall Center, add it to the GCCMA section regardless of which other MOC section it also belongs in. This mirrors the FOL dual-link rule and the JapanTrip folder rule.
