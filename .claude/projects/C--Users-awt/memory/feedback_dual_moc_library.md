When a note's content is directly relevant to libraries as institutions (not just incidentally mentioning them), link it in both its primary topical MOC and in `MOC - Friends of the Georgetown Public Library` / `## Overview`.

**Why:** *Palaces for the People* was classified only to Social Issues; user corrected that it should also appear in FOL MOC because the book's core argument (libraries as social infrastructure) is directly relevant to FOL's mission.

**How to apply:** During classify, if a note's content makes a substantive argument about libraries — their civic value, operational models, community role, or social impact — add a second MOC link to FOL / ## Overview in addition to the primary subject MOC. Operational FOL content (LGL, procedures) still goes under its specific FOL subsection only.
